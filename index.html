Welcome to Scrubby's Laundromat

Scrubby's Laundromat has been in business for over 27 years

When you come to Scrubbys, you will find the best equipment that the industry has to offer.

Commercial Washers and Dryers

We carry both Dexter and Maytag washers and dryers for your convenience. All large capacity washers are great for comforters (including Queen and King), sleeping bags and anything to big to wash at home.

Washers

  • Dexter Mega 60LBS (6 loads)
  • Dexter Maxi 40LBS (4 loads)
  • Dexter Triple loaders
  • Maytag Single top loaders

Dryers

  • 16 Dexter dryers (3 loads)
  • Single Load Maytag Dryers

Services We Offer

Drop Off Service

Don't have time to do laundry. Just leave it with us.

We will sort, wash, dry, fold and follow any special instructions you give us.

Same day or next day service available.

Folding Service

Do you need us to watch your laundry, while you run errands. No problem. We can transfer loads in/out of washers/dryers for you.

Folding services are provided on site.

Dry Cleaning

Need to run to the Dry Cleaners? Scrubbys is your one-stop shop for all your dry cleaning needs.

Next day service available (not available after 7pm)

For your convenience, we provide Wi-Fi, televisions and snacks. You will be pleasantly surprise with our prices. Any needed coin can be purchase with Interac, Visa or Mastercard.
